Gabányi Anna [panka]
vettem hollandiában bols advocaatot, és most bontottam ki. és kiderült, hogy a sűrű, kanalazható fajtát hoztam. valami isteni!!!
There are two varieties of Bols Advocaat: a "thick" Bols Advocaat which is sold mostly on the Dutch market and eaten with a spoon, and the more liquid "export" version. The thick Bols Advocaat also contains egg white, while in the export version only the pure egg yolks are used.
Use
Thick Bols Advocaat has traditionally enjoyed popularity, not only as an aperitif or digestif, but also as an ingredient for all kinds of desserts, for instance with icecreams and pastries. The export variety is particularly suited for cocktails and longdrinks.
The traditional thick Bols Advocaat was, and still is to a large extent, enjoyed in a wide glass. Fill the glass to about 2/3 and top it with a liberal amount of sweetened whipped cream. A sprinkling of cocoa powder or vanilla sugar makes this traditional Dutch treat all the more enjoyable.
