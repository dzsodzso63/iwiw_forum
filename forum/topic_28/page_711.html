Makrobiotikus lehet az, mert ezt találtam:
What are probiotics?
Probiotics are bacterial cultures that support a good and healthy intestinal bacterial flora. They will give you the means to maintain a balanced bacterial flora in the intestine. This is a predisposition for your health - but it is important in the beginning to be cautious and take a low dosage. If you overdose you may experience an excessive drainage syndrome, causing side effects such as headache, diarrhea or constipation. If a reaction is experienced the dosage should be lowered, then gradually built up until a balance is reached at a sustainable level. Some people experience excessive production of gas. This is individual and normally lasts for some days. You ought to adapt to a low dosage in the first weeks and later on increase the dosage.
