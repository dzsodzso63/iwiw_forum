On Driving Test
In West London a man eas taking a driving tes to get his license to drive his motor scooter. At the test center, he was given the instructions they always gave out: He was supposed to drive on a specific route. To determine his driving skill, he would be observed by a hidden examiner. And at some point during the drive this hidden examiner would suddenly appear and step in front of the scooter. This would test his braking skills.
Easy enough. Tha man dutifully followed the instructions, driving carefully through the route. But when he reached the end of it, the examiner had not appeared in front of the scooter. So the man drove through the route another time. Then another. Still no examiner.
Finally the man pulledup to the test center and asked what was going on. He had driven through the route three times and had not seen the examiner. Where was he?
"We are sorry," he was told. "He stepped in front of the wrong scooter."
